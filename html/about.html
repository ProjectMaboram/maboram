Vilka är vi!

Welcome to Maboram, your number one source for all frames. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of frames, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 1800 by Miss Daniel Maboram, we have come a long way from our beginnings in a tent in the woods. When Miss Daniel first started out, her passion for helping other people be more eco-friendly, providing the best equipment for her fellow wifes drove her to do intense research, quit her day job, and gave her the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade picture frame industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us. Sincerely, Maboram.
